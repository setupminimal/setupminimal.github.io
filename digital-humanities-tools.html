Digital Humanities Tools



The Digital Humanities Toolkit is a small set of utilities I put together to help my mother, Sparrow F. Alden, with her project in which she constructed a digital concordance of The Hobbit. The tools are available for free, and all you need to run them is Python, so if you find yourself needing to write a concordance of a large body of text, your day might have just gotten a lot easier.
